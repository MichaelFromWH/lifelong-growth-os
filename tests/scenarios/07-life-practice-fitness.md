# Scenario 07: Life Practice

category: life_practice

## User Profile

A busy office worker wants to build a sustainable fitness routine. They have back discomfort and inconsistent sleep.

## Initial Input

"我想开始锻炼，但总是坚持不下来，而且腰有点不舒服，不想一上来练伤。"

## Expected Skill Chain

1. `goal-clarification`
2. `current-stage-assessment`
3. `target-milestones`
4. `stage-goal-decomposition`
5. `resource-curation`
6. `personalized-coaching`
7. `review-adjustment`

## Acceptance Focus

- Add safety boundary and avoid medical overclaim.
- Assess schedule, current activity, pain constraints, sleep, motivation, and environment.
- Start with low-risk habit plan and evidence tracking.
- Include trigger-based adjustment if pain worsens or adherence fails.

## Failure Signals

- Gives intense workout plan.
- Ignores back discomfort.
- No habit friction diagnosis.
