# Scenario 06: Career Growth

category: career_growth

## User Profile

A traditional product manager wants to transition into AI product management within two months.

## Initial Input

"我想转 AI 产品经理，但我不知道自己到底缺什么，也不知道该怎么证明自己有这个能力。"

## Expected Skill Chain

1. `goal-clarification`
2. `current-stage-assessment`
3. `target-milestones`
4. `stage-goal-decomposition`
5. `resource-curation`
6. `personalized-coaching`
7. `review-adjustment`

## Acceptance Focus

- Define target role and proof artifacts such as case study, prototype, prompt workflow, or portfolio.
- Assess product fundamentals, AI literacy, technical collaboration, data thinking, and agent workflow understanding.
- Map capability gaps to concrete artifacts.
- Plan should create proof-of-work, not just knowledge consumption.

## Failure Signals

- Recommends only AI news and courses.
- No portfolio artifact.
- Ignores the user's existing product experience.
